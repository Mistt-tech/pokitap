Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

Pokitap ("the Game") is a free mobile game developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

By playing Pokitap, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Pokitap does not directly collect any personally identifiable information. However, the following third-party services integrated in the Game may collect certain data:

  • Unity Ads – used to display optional rewarded ads. Unity may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Unity's Privacy Policy.
  • Leaderboard service (jsonbin.io) – stores your high score anonymously. No name or personal data is required.

How Information Is Used

  • To display rewarded advertisements (Unity Ads)
  • To store and display global high scores anonymously
  • To improve gameplay experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the services listed above.

Advertising

Pokitap shows rewarded ads powered by Unity Ads. Ads are only shown when the player chooses to watch them (e.g., to revive). We do not show interstitial or banner ads automatically.

Unity Ads may use your device's advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Children's Privacy

Pokitap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Data Retention & Security

Leaderboard scores are stored anonymously and can be cleared upon request. We take reasonable precautions to protect data, but no system is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
